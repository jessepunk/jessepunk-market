Jessepunk is more than an NFT marketplace — it is a growing digital economy built on Base. $JESSE is the native token powering governance, rewards, identity, and community-owned infrastructure.

Just like BILLIONS.Network positions $BILL as the currency of the AI-powered human economy, Jessepunk positions $JESSE as the cultural and economic engine for collectors, artists, traders, and builders across the Base ecosystem.

Jessepunk is built on three core principles:

  • Transparency — fully on-chain, open vault, visible allocations
  • Community — NFTs unlock ownership, airdrops, and influence
  • Value Flywheel — market volume → rewards → growth → token utility

Project Overview

Jessepunk is a decentralized NFT marketplace built for the Base ecosystem. Designed with transparency, security, and community ownership at its core, Jessepunk introduces a powerful token model, community airdrops, and long-term roadmap supporting builders and traders alike.

FAQ

What is the Vault?

The Vault stores 10% of the token supply for long-term utility. It is protected by a multisig and timelock for maximum transparency.

How does the airdrop work?

Once all NFTs are minted, the airdrop will be rolled out within 3–7 days. Legendary holders get the largest share.

What chain is this on?

Jessepunk is launching exclusively on Base.
